264  /// Total number of muffin-tin and plane-wave expansion coefficients for the wave-functions.
265  /** APW+lo basis \f$ \varphi_{\mu {\bf k}}({\bf r}) = \{ \varphi_{\bf G+k}({\bf r}),
266  * \varphi_{j{\bf k}}({\bf r}) \} \f$ is used to expand first-variational wave-functions:
267  *
268  * \f[
269  * \psi_{i{\bf k}}({\bf r}) = \sum_{\mu} c_{\mu i}^{\bf k} \varphi_{\mu \bf k}({\bf r}) =
270  * \sum_{{\bf G}}c_{{\bf G} i}^{\bf k} \varphi_{\bf G+k}({\bf r}) +
271  * \sum_{j}c_{j i}^{\bf k}\varphi_{j{\bf k}}({\bf r})
272  * \f]
273  *
274  * Inside muffin-tins the expansion is converted into the following form:
275  * \f[
276  * \psi_{i {\bf k}}({\bf r})= \begin{array}{ll}

280  * \f]
281  *

283  * to the number of muffin-tin basis functions of the form \f$ f_{\ell \lambda}^{\alpha}(r)

591 /** \page basis Basis functions for Kohn-Sham wave-functions expansion
592  *
593  * \section basis1 LAPW+lo basis
594  *
595  * LAPW+lo basis consists of two different sets of functions: LAPW functions \f$ \varphi_{{\bf G+k}} \f$ defined over
596  * entire unit cell:
597  * \f[
598  * \varphi_{{\bf G+k}}({\bf r}) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}
599  * \displaystyle \sum_{L} \sum_{\nu=1}^{O_{\ell}^{\alpha}} a_{L\nu}^{\alpha}({\bf G+k})u_{\ell \nu}^{\alpha}(r)
600  * Y_{\ell m}(\hat {\bf r}) & {\bf r} \in {\rm MT} \alpha \\
601  * \displaystyle \frac{1}{\sqrt \Omega} e^{i({\bf G+k}){\bf r}} & {\bf r} \in {\rm I} \end{array} \right.
602  * \f]
603  * and Bloch sums of local orbitals defined inside muffin-tin spheres only:
604  * \f[
605  * \begin{array}{ll} \displaystyle \varphi_{j{\bf k}}({\bf r})=\sum_{{\bf T}} e^{i{\bf kT}}
606  * \varphi_{j}({\bf r - T}) & {\rm {\bf r} \in MT} \end{array}
607  * \f]
608  * Each local orbital is composed of radial and angular parts:
609  * \f[
610  * \varphi_{j}({\bf r}) = \phi_{\ell_j}^{\zeta_j,\alpha_j}(r) Y_{\ell_j m_j}(\hat {\bf r})
611  * \f]
612  * Radial part of local orbital is defined as a linear combination of radial functions (minimum two radial functions
613  * are required) such that local orbital vanishes at the sphere boundary:
614  * \f[
615  * \phi_{\ell}^{\zeta, \alpha}(r) = \sum_{p}\gamma_{p}^{\zeta,\alpha} u_{\ell \nu_p}^{\alpha}(r)
616  * \f]
617  *
618  * Arbitrary number of local orbitals can be introduced for each angular quantum number (this is highlighted by
619  * the index \f$ \zeta \f$).
620  *
621  * Radial functions are m-th order (with zero-order being a function itself) energy derivatives of the radial
622  * Schrdinger equation:
623  * \f[
624  * u_{\ell \nu}^{\alpha}(r) = \frac{\partial^{m_{\nu}}}{\partial^{m_{\nu}}E}u_{\ell}^{\alpha}(r,E)\Big|_{E=E_{\nu}}
625  * \f]
626  */
627
